Ch 3 — Risk Assessment & Internal Control (SA 315, 320, 330)
Audit Risk Formula
FormulaComponents
Audit Risk= Risk of Material Misstatement × Detection Risk
RMM= Inherent Risk × Control Risk
∴ Audit Risk= IR × CR × DR
